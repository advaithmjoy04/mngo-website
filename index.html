FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

What is MNGO?
MNGO is a campus placement platform that helps colleges, students, TPOs and recruiters manage placement drives from a single system.
How does MNGO help TPOs?
Automated notifications, application tracking, analytics, reporting and recruiter management reduce manual work.
Can recruiters post jobs directly?
Yes. Recruiters can create drives, filter eligible candidates and track applications.
Is MNGO suitable for Tier 2 and Tier 3 colleges?
Yes. The platform is designed specifically to simplify campus placements for growing institutions.
